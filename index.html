A Comprehensive Digital Shield.

CYBER SHELL provides multi-layered protection against a wide range of digital threats.

Instant Link & Message Checking

Our core feature. We analyze URLs for phishing, malware, and scam indicators using a combination of Google Safe Browsing, VirusTotal, and our own AI pattern detection.

AI-Powered Fake News Verification

Combat misinformation. We check forwarded news against credible fact-checking APIs like Alt News and BOOM Live, supplemented by our growing community-flagged database.

Community-Driven Scam Reporting

Leverage the power of the community. Check suspicious phone numbers against a user-generated database of reported spam and fraud, similar to Truecaller but for all digital threats.
