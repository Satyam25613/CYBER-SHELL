Frequently Asked Questions

Is CYBER SHELL completely free to use?

Yes, our core features—link, message, and number checking—are and will always be free for individual citizens. We plan to offer advanced features for businesses and institutions in the future.

How does the AI know if something is a scam?

Our AI is trained on millions of examples of fraudulent messages and links. It learns to recognize patterns, keywords, and structures commonly used in scams, even in regional Indian languages.

Do you read or store my personal messages?

No. Your privacy is our top priority. When you forward a message, our system only analyzes the content for threats. We do not store personal conversations or link them to your identity.

What platforms can I use CYBER SHELL on?

Currently, our MVP is live on Telegram. We are actively developing a dedicated mobile app for both Android and iOS, as well as a WhatsApp bot for even easier access.
